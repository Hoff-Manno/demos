4. Implementation Plan

A Phased Approach

Phase1

Audit & Data Mapping

  • Inventory all documents, reports, and tools they already use.
  • Map where the data lives and how it flows.
Phase2

MVP Build

  • Start small with one RAG assistant trained on past project briefs.
  • Pilot it with a few account managers.
Phase3

Integrations

  • Connect ad platforms and reporting pipelines.
  • Build the client portal once the backend is stable.
Phase4

Empower the Team

  • Run workshops: ‘Here’s how to ask better questions of the AI.’
  • Provide playbooks and Loom tutorials so adoption sticks.

5. Long-Term Vision

Adoption, Measurement & Growth

Onboarding & Training

Group sessions, cheat sheets, and Loom walkthroughs will ensure the team is confident and capable from day one.

Measuring Success

Success will be measured by tracking key metrics like hours saved, reduction in reporting errors, and client satisfaction scores.

Scaling

Once the core system is proven, we will expand the AI assistant to include new data sources, enhance the client portal, and enable deeper, predictive reporting.
